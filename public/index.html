ZOMBIE ATTACK
The higher the density, the lower the chances of surviving a Zombie attack. Population density will be the most important factor to determine the survival index.
Children should be more careful, Zombies like fresh flesh and will be on the outlook for young humans.
Zombies are attracted by wealthy and luxurious areas, hence why living in an area where the average income is high will be more disastrous.
FIRE BREAK OUT
When a fire breaks out, people are safer in remote places.
In this scenario, the chances of survival are lower in cities where the population density is higher. Wise people are more likely to find an escape from a big fire.
Water proximity is the most important survival factor, water being a main resource to calm down fires.
EARTHQUAKE
Earthquakes tend to be happening in the same area, history of earthquakes in the selected suburb will have a high impact.
However this may be balanced by living in an area where the average income is higher or the number of hospitals is high.
Those suburbs tend to be more prepared to earthquake disasters. Smaller and younger individuals are more flexible and will be more able to protect themselves by hiding in small places.
