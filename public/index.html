ZOMBIE ATTACK
The higher the density, the lower the chances of surviving a Zombie attack. Population density will be the most important factor to determine the survival index. Children should be more careful, Zombies like fresh flesh and will be on the outlook for young humans. Zombies are attracted by wealthy and luxurious areas, hence why living in an area where the average income is high will be more disastrous. FIRE BREAK OUT When a fire breaks out, people are safer in cities where the population density is higher. In this scenario, if living in a remote place surrounded by bushes, the chances of survival are lower. Wise people are more likely to find an escape from a big fire. Water proximity is the most important survival factor, water being a main resource to calm down fires.EARTHQUAKE Earthquakes are usually happening in the same areas. If an earthquake happened in the area before, the population will be highly affected. However this may be balanced by living in an area where the average income is higher. Suburbs with a higher income tend to be more prepared to earthquake disasters. Smaller individuals are more flexible and will be more able to protect themselves by hiding in small places, younger individuals are thus more likely to survive. Finally, having hospitals close is important to survive in case of a massive earthquake.
